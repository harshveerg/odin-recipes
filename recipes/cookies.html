Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies, what a classic. These are very easy to make and this is my go to recipe to make whenever I'm craving a quick snack. These can be made in under 45 minutes, including the prep time, bake time, and cooling. They only require a few ingredients. I thought about making some while writing this guide! The taste is amazing, the texture is perfect and fluffy, and the speed at which you can make these at is the cherry on top!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • salt
  • 1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips (as desired, but don't put too many!)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C. Place parchment paper on two baking sheets and set aside.
  2. Combine dry ingredients (not the sugars) in a large mixing bowl. Whisk the flour, baking soda, and salt to ensure even mixture.
  3. Combine wet ingredients (and sugars). Use a mixer on medium-high speed to beat the butter and sugars together in a separate bowl. Mix until light and fluffy. Add eggs one by one, beating the mixture before adding in the second egg. Lastly, add the vanilla extract.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the mixing bowl, and use the mixer at a reduced speed to combine them.
  5. Once a dough has been made, add the chocolate chips to the bowl and use a utensil to hand stir
  6. Scoop out 12 evenly sized, heaping spoons of dough onto each lined baking sheet, roll each scoop into a ball. Evenly space your cookies and bake! If there's any left over dough, bake it at the end after your baking sheets have cooled down from the first batch of cookies.
  7. After 6-8 minutes, rotate your baking sheets to ensure even heat distribution all cookies for optimal consistency. Bake until golden brown, usually 12-15 minutes.
  8. Let your cookies cool down on the baking sheet for a couple minutes before transferring to a cooling rack to completely cool down. Once cool, you can enjoy your cookies right away or store them in an air-tight container for up to a week.

I hope you enjoyed them as much as I did!
